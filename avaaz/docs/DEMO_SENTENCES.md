# Demo sentences (use these for a smooth live demo)

Use these when showing the app. They match the rules and lexicon so gloss and videos play correctly.

---

## Opening (greetings)

- Hello
- Hi
- Hello, how are you?
- How are you?

---

## Name & intro

- What is your name?
- My name is Ashik
- My name is Reshma
- I am Priya
- So, what is your name? My name is Ashik Joy.

---

## Where you live

- I live in Angamaly

## Two-part (sentence splitting)

- Hello, how are you? My name is Ashik.
- What is your name? My name is Reshma.
- Thank you. Bye.
- I am happy. I live in Kochi.
- Where do you live? I live in Mumbai.

---

## Tips for demo

1. **Pause clearly** after each sentence so ASR cuts at the right place.
2. **Stick to the list** above to avoid missing-token warnings.
3. **Names/places** (e.g. Ashik, Joy, Kochi, Angamaly) are fingerspelled automatically.
4. Keep sentences **short** for best accuracy.
